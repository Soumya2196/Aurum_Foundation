About Us

Aurum Foundation is an NGO working since 2000 in the fields of public health, social empowerment, women and child development, education and agriculture. We are based out of a remote village in Odisha and our goal is to empower people from poor and marginalised communities, thereby improving their livelihoods. We believe in the government‚Äôs vision of sustainable development and adhering to its principles have organised various development projects, workshops, training camps and programs.

Our Mission

To make a country of hope,wisdom and social justice, where the problems of people is overcome and people live with dignity and security.

Our Vision

Aurum Foundation helps reduce social exclusion by accelerating education, health and empowerment for people in rural areas.

Our Plans

We plan on providing better lifestyle to people especially children those need constant care during their growth period. We aim to provide basic education to every individual that our foundation can take under its wing. We plan to provide the basic needs of life till one can be independent.
